A summary of a Zillow and Redfin comparison SA article by Dan Owens.

Both positive quarterly earnings in the third quarter 2020. Not profitable yearly though.

Housing demand surge by COVID can be a reason.

How to be profitable remains questionable.

Both offers two choices: sell directly to third parity or sell to company.

Direct buying (by the company for resale) services are called Instant Offer and RedfinNow

They are quite similar.

(In November 2020) Zillow is priced at a premium.

What’s disruptive about these two is the direct home buying service?

However, Zillow appears to be expanding faster than Redfin on this, meaning that Redfin is less of a pure play on this disruptive concept.
