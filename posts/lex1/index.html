At a result, to those philosophical and vague hypothesis/questions without substantial answers, often the listener’s reaction is like “but ’ve also thought about before (I’m here for an answer)”.

Still, there are occationally good ideas that at least can serve as a seed, or starting point, for self meditation, which could potentially lead to some fruitful inner conclusion.

The first clip I pick up is the on with Aaron Brooks.

The subject: What are the principles of a life well lived?

  • To live with thought, to live rational life, to think it thru
  • I think lots of people live like zombie, Their mind is not focused on “what do I need to do”
  • Most of people go thru the motion of living rather than embrace
  • The secret is to take it seriously
  • Use the one tool that provides value: our mind or reason
  • Apply it to the living. People use it in work, math, programming… But imagine if they use that same energy same focus to actually live life - and choose value that they should pursue.
  • We have different way to condition our consciousness: “I am serious now” *wears suit and tie
  • Every minute is valuable. People don’t have respect for the time and their mind.
  • People do amazing jobs in work. But what if they apply that to everything in life.
  • Experiencing moment vs thinking through:
  • Experiencing is the easy part. The problem is people don’t use their mind in planning their life, in thinking about how to live. There is no other source of knowledge: we don’t know anything other from the sense of our mind. Reasoning is how human survive.
  • We are not programmed to hunt, to do agriculture, or to program. All of that requires focus, energy, will, and choice. Once you make that - choice, you have to engage, and in work to.
  • What’s screwing up the world is that people have the wrong values, they don’t think about them, don’t focus, don’t make plan about life.
  • We have the ability to reject our mind and reasoning (e.g. suicide). To focus or to not focus.
  • It requires an act of will to say I got do something waking up.
  • You have to turn something on. This is not determined. You choose it.
  • We don’t really know how brain works, what is efficent. But the beauty of human brain is the potentialities that we have to manifest through our choices. How we evoke different senses.
