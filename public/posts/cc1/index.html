So can I do this?

Looking into my toolbox, the only thing that somewhat links to art and creativity is deep learning.

Question: What can deep learning do in creative coding/art?

This is obviously not a new question at all. We have all those generative models like conditional gans that turn pictures into art works.

But what I’m trying to do is to somewhat mimic the style of the works in the previous section of this writing.

One thing that comes to mind immediately is a GRN and VAE based model called Sketch-RNN.

It’s interesting because it generates strokes instead of pixels.

How are strokes represented?

  • stroke-5 representation: (Δx, Δy, p1, p2, p3)
  • Δx, Δy are change from the previous point
  • p1, p2, and p3 are one hot representation of three states:
    1. touching paper (line from curr to next)
    2. lifted
    3. drawing ends
